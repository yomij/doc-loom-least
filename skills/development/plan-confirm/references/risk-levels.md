# Risk Levels

| Level | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `low` | Small, reversible, local, well-verified; no meaningful authority, public, private-data, money, or destructive effect. |
| `medium` | Reversible internal behavior/Skill/API/data-flow or broader local change with bounded recovery. |
| `high` | Security, money, private data, irreversible/destructive action, public compatibility, L1 critical fact, or authorization weakening. |

Classify by consequence, reversibility, exposure, authority impact, and
verification. A sensitive topic still triggers context checking even when final
execution risk is medium.

One-shot low risk uses no case; verified Fast-Path records agent approval;
other low/medium plans need confirmation; high risk needs explicit confirmation
of the current object. Fast-Path must satisfy every shared condition and never
becomes background permission.
